Privacy Policy

Boss Mode Live Score Ticker· Last updated: June 2026

This extension is operated as an independent product. We designed it to collect as little data as possible.

What we collect

  • Local settings — Boss Mode state, notification preferences, and cached match scores are stored locally in your browser via Chrome storage. This data stays on your device.
  • Payment information — If you purchase Premium, payment is handled by ExtensionPay and Stripe. We receive confirmation of your paid status, not your card details. See ExtensionPay's privacy policy.
  • Match data requests — Premium users fetch live scores from our cache server. These requests contain no personally identifiable information.

What we do not collect

  • Browsing history or page content
  • Personal identity beyond what Stripe/ExtensionPay processes during checkout
  • Location data

Third-party services

  • ExtensionPay / Stripe — Premium payments
  • API-Football (via our cache server) — Live match scores for Premium users
  • VPN affiliate links — Optional links in the extension footer; clicking them leaves the extension

Data retention

Local extension data persists until you uninstall the extension or clear extension storage. Payment records are retained by Stripe per their policies.
